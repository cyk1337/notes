Posted on 2024-05-10 In Transformer , Attention Views: Disqus:

Efficient attention mechanisms are crucial for scaling transformers in large-scale applications. Here we explore different attention variants of Multi-Head Attention (MHA), Multi-Query Attention (MQA), Grouped-Query Attention (GQA), and Multi-Head Latent Attention (MLA), analyzing their trade-offs in memory, speed, and expressivity, and how they enhance transformer scalability. ðŸš€
